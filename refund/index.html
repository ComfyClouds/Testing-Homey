1. Inspection Before Delivery

  • You have the option to open and inspect your order upon delivery to confirm the quality and material of the items.

2. Exchange Policy

  • Size Exchanges: If you need a different size for the same design, exchanges are available within inspecting your order, as long as the requested size is in stock.
  • Condition: To qualify for an exchange, the item must be in its original condition — unused, unwashed, and undamaged.
  • Packaging: Please ensure the item is securely placed in its original packaging and hand it to the delivery representative when exchanging.
  • Shipping Fees: Additional shipping fees apply for exchanges.

3. Refund Policy

  • No Refunds: Once the order has been accepted, refunds are not available.
  • Complete Returns Only: Orders cannot be returned partially. The entire order must be exchanged or returned together.

4. Refusing to Receive the Order

If you choose to refuse the order, you are required to cover the shipping costs by paying them directly to Homey Vibes. Please note the following details:

4-1. Payment of Shipping Costs

  • Shipping costs must be transferred directly to Homey Vibes and not to the delivery agent.
  • Any payment made to the delivery agent will be treated as a tip and will not count toward the shipping costs.

4-2. Available Payment Methods for Return Shipping

  • You can pay via InstaPay or Vodafone Cash.
